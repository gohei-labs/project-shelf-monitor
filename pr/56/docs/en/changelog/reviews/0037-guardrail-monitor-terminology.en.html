Guardrail Monitor Terminology

RecommendedGovernanceMonitor

Decision Summary

Retire "guardrail map" as current terminology and standardize on "guardrail monitor" for the generated browser-visible page.

What The Owner Needs To Approve

Approve monitor vocabulary: source files hold the truth, and generated monitor pages reflect that truth accurately for human oversight.

Recommendation

Proceed. "Monitor" better matches the human job: watching project state, spotting drift, and deciding what needs attention.

Risks And Mitigations

RiskMitigation
Generated HTML drifts from the source.`check-registry.ps1` verifies that `monitor/guardrail-monitor*.html` matches `monitor/registry.json`.
Historical records still say "map".Keep old review titles as history, but remove the term from current docs, scripts, generated pages, and navigation.
Future monitors invent inconsistent naming.Use the pattern: one source of truth plus generated monitor output.
